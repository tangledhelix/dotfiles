Spell check

    :set spell!     Toggle spell check mode (also bound to ,s)
    ]s              Next error
    [s              Previous error
    z=              List of suggestions
    zg              Add to my dictionary (zug to undo)
    zw              Remove from my dictionary (zuw to undo)
